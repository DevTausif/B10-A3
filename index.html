Design and Project Management Stages

Concept Phase: This is where your ideas and vision start to take shape. The focus is on understanding the client's needs and translating them into creative concepts. This phase involves brainstorming, sketching, and exploring different design possibilities to establish a clear direction for the project.

Design Process: Once the concept is finalized, the design process begins. This involves refining ideas into detailed plans, selecting materials, creating digital models, and ensuring that the design is both beautiful and functional. Collaboration with the client is key to making sure the design aligns with their vision.

Supervision and Execution:During the construction phase, supervision ensures that the design is executed perfectly. Close on-site management helps to coordinate contractors, monitor progress, and address any challenges, ensuring that the final result stays true to the original design.
